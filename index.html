About Me

I graduated from Georgia Southern University in May 2016 with a Bachelor of General Studies degree with an emphasis in Information Systems and moved to Jacksonville, Florida immediately after. I was unable to find work in my field in Jacksonville, so I worked several medical front office jobs until I accepted an Office Automation position at Naval Hospital Jacksonville in January 2021. Not quite IS, but it was on the right track and helped revive my excitement for information management.

Six months after starting that position, my husband was transferred and we moved from Florida to Maryland.
